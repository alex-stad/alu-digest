Novelis Wins Award for 100% Recycled End-Of-Life Vehicle Scrap Product

Novelis received the MMK Award of Excellence 2026 on March 11 for aluminium sheet produced from 100% end-of-life vehicle (ELV) scrap, demonstrating technical feasibility of high-quality exterior car body applications from closed-loop recycled material. The award validates Novelis's 3x30 car-to-car recycling strategy and strengthens its competitive positioning with automotive OEM customers including JLR, BMW, and Audi, who face growing pressure to increase recycled content in body-in-white sheet.

Constellium Board Authorizes a New $300 million Share Repurchase Program Expiring in December 2028

Constellium's board authorized a new $300 million share repurchase program on March 12, effective after its May 21 AGM through December 2028, replacing a 2024 buyback authorization and following its second-best-ever annual results of $8.4 billion revenue (+15% YoY) and 1.5 Mt shipments. Trade Policy Aluminium International Today · 19 Mar #3

Alvance increases aluminium production by 10%

Alvance British Aluminium boosted output at its Lochaber smelter (UK's only primary plant, 48 kt/yr) by approximately 10% after the UK secured a preferential 25% US tariff rate versus the 50% applied globally, allowing the facility to export roughly half its production to US markets for the first time. The tariff-driven trade flow realignment illustrates how US Section 232 duties are reshaping North American aluminium supply routes and tightening primary metal availability — dynamics that affect input costs for Novelis's US rolling operations, including the Bay Minette ramp-up.

Alcoa reports higher orders following Middle East aluminium supply disruption

Alcoa reported increased orders on March 20 as Middle East supply disruptions redirected approximately 4 million tonnes per year of Gulf-region-bound metal into alternative markets, with analysts projecting a global aluminium deficit of approximately 1.9 million tonnes for 2026. The supply tightening supports elevated LME prices and widens the cost advantage of Novelis's high-recycled-content production model, as scrap-based FRP becomes increasingly competitive versus primary-reliant rivals.

Energy & Upstream Aluminium International Today · 17 Mar #5

Mozal aluminium smelter to be placed on care and maintenance from March 2026

South32 placed its 560,000 tonne/year Mozal smelter in Mozambique under care and maintenance from March 15 after failing to secure an affordable electricity supply agreement, permanently removing one of the largest single blocks of primary aluminium capacity from the global market. Mozal's closure accelerates the 2026 supply deficit, reinforcing the upward pressure on LME prices already driven by Middle East disruptions, and improving the economics of scrap-based production which underpins Novelis's 3x30 recycled content targets.
