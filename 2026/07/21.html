Steel Dynamics' aluminium flat-rolled mill achieves full 650,000 t/year capacity; automotive qualifications secured

Steel Dynamics completed and commenced commissioning of its third and final cold mill at its Columbus, Mississippi aluminium flat-rolled facility in July 2026, bringing total annual capacity to 650,000 metric tonnes per year; the company confirmed receipt of automotive aluminium sheet product qualifications, with commercial automotive sales expected to begin before end of 2026.

AAI urges duty safeguards and BIS scrap standards as India's aluminium imports rise to 3.48 MTPA

India's Aluminium Association of India urged the Ministry of Finance to impose safeguard import duties and establish BIS quality standards for aluminium scrap imports, citing a rise in total aluminium imports to 3.48 million tonnes per annum that the association says is undercutting domestic producers.

Novelis angle

Safeguard duties on flat-rolled semi-finished imports would support Novelis India's competitive position; BIS scrap quality standards could tighten secondary feedstock availability and raise recycled-content costs for Indian FRP producers including Novelis.
