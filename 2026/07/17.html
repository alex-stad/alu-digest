Kotak upgrades Hindalco to 'Buy', sees 17% upside on aluminium and Novelis recovery

Kotak Institutional Equities upgraded Hindalco from 'Reduce' to 'Buy' with a revised target of INR 1,120 per share (17% upside), citing improving earnings visibility, Novelis' Oswego hot mill restart, healthier US scrap spreads, and a projected global aluminium supply deficit of 900,000 tonnes in 2026. The brokerage values Hindalco at approximately 5.5x FY28 EV/EBITDA following a recent correction in the share price.

Stellantis Q2 2026 estimated consolidated shipments of 1.6 million units, +10% year-over-year

Stellantis reported estimated Q2 2026 consolidated shipments of 1.6 million units, up 10% year-over-year, driven by North America (+38% YoY on Ram and Jeep refreshes) and Enlarged Europe (+5% YoY with BEV as primary driver). Full financial results are scheduled for release on 30 July 2026.

Novelis angle

A 10% annual rebound in Stellantis shipments is a direct positive signal for automotive BIW aluminium sheet and battery enclosure demand from one of Novelis's largest European OEM customers, reinforcing the H2 2026 demand outlook.

Industry News 16 Jul #3

China's NFC produces first aluminium at Vietnam smelter

NFC's Dak Nong smelter in Vietnam tapped its first molten primary aluminium on 16 July, 50 days ahead of the contractual milestone. The 450,000 tpy facility enters Phase 1 at 150,000 tpy, ramping to full capacity by 2027–2028, becoming Vietnam's first large-scale electrolytic aluminium plant.

Novelis angle

While Phase 1 output is modest at 150 kt, the full 450 kt ramp adds a new Southeast Asian P1020 source that could soften European aluminium premiums over the medium term as geopolitical supply constraints ease.
