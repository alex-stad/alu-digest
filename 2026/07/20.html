Constellium to source local renewable energy for its German facilities

Constellium signed a 10-year power purchase agreement with the community of Gottmadingen to receive the full output of the Katzental solar park for its Gottmadingen and Singen plants in Germany, covering over 25% of combined electricity needs and expected to avoid approximately 3,500 tCO₂/year from spring 2027.

Novelis angle

Constellium's long-term solar PPA at German plants advances its low-carbon credentials; Novelis's Nachterstedt and Göttingen plants will face similar customer and sustainability reporting expectations to demonstrate equivalent renewable-energy sourcing.
