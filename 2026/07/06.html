Adani and IHC unveil $11.5 billion Odisha aluminium complex with 4–5 year timeline

Adani Enterprises and Abu Dhabi's IHC (International Resources Holding) signed a $11.5 billion 50:50 JV MoU to build India's largest aluminium complex in Odisha — comprising a 4 Mt/yr alumina refinery, a 2 Mt/yr primary smelter, and a 1 Mt/yr downstream park — representing the largest-ever foreign direct investment in the Indian metals sector.
