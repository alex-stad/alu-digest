EOS partners with Constellium on aluminium alloys for additive manufacturing

EOS and Constellium announced a long-term strategic partnership to co-develop and market advanced aluminium alloys for industrial 3D printing, adding Constellium's proprietary Aheadd CP1 and Al5X1 alloys to the EOS metal AM systems portfolio with validated process parameters for reliable industrial-scale production.

Novelis angle

Constellium's move to co-brand proprietary alloys through an AM platform partner signals a strategy to monetise alloy IP across manufacturing routes beyond flat-rolled; Novelis has not publicly entered the AM materials licensing space.

Sustainability & Recycling 9 Jul #3

ASI certifies Amcor Flexibles Sélestat against Performance Standard V3.1

The Aluminium Stewardship Initiative (ASI) certified Amcor Flexibles Sélestat (France) against the ASI Performance Standard V3.1 (2023), extending responsible sourcing verification to a major European flexible packaging converter serving the food and beverage sector.

Novelis angle

Growing ASI certification among European packaging converters strengthens chain-of-custody traceability and supports Novelis's ability to sell certified-sustainable FRP at premium pricing to buyers with ESG procurement mandates.

Trade & Regulation 8 Jul #4

Aluminum Association calls for comprehensive strategy to strengthen US aluminium supply chain

The Aluminum Association published a policy roadmap urging US policymakers to adopt an 'all of the above' approach combining new primary smelting capacity, recycled aluminium investment, and stronger trade enforcement against state-sponsored overcapacity to secure domestic aluminium supply chains.

Novelis angle

The roadmap's emphasis on recycling investment and trade enforcement directly supports the economic case for Novelis's Bay Minette plant, where contracted beverage packaging volumes depend on tariff protection against non-market-economy FRP imports.
