SACA, a new coalition, debuts to support growth of recycled aluminium production in the US

The Secondary Aluminum Coalition for America (SACA), representing scrap collection, remelting, extrusion and fabrication companies across 17 US states, conducted a Washington fly-in to meet White House, DOE, ITA, USTR and Congressional officials, presenting secondary aluminium as the fastest and lowest-cost path to expanding US domestic supply; the sector accounts for more than 97% of US aluminium industry employment.

Novelis angle

As the world’s largest aluminium recycler and a major US secondary producer, Novelis stands to benefit from any policy framework that prioritises recycled-content incentives over primary capacity—directly supporting the economics of the Bay Minette greenfield.
