Bridgnorth Aluminium secures five-year battery foil supply deal with Lotte Aluminium Materials USA

Bridgnorth Aluminium (BAL), the UK's last remaining aluminium rolling mill, signed a five-year supply agreement with Lotte Aluminium Materials USA to deliver battery-grade aluminium foil for the EV battery sector; the contract was formalised in Elizabethtown, Kentucky and covers output from BAL's 368-person Shropshire facility.

Aluminum's war shock blunted by dark transits and Chinese supply

The Iran war triggered one of aluminium's largest supply shocks, but the feared surge above $4,000/t did not materialise: Gulf smelters kept alumina flowing through the Strait of Hormuz via 'dark transit' vessels that disabled AIS tracking, with Persian Gulf alumina imports returning to pre-war levels by May 2026, while Chinese smelters operating at an annualised ~47 Mt — above the official 45 Mt cap — provided an additional supply buffer.

Novelis angle

With the Hormuz peace framework signed 19 June, the risk premium embedded in European P1020 premiums should continue unwinding in H2 2026, reducing a key metal-cost headwind for Novelis's European plants.
