Sustainability & Recycling 10 Jun #3

Germany's aluminium recycling output drops 3% year-on-year; recovery looks lean in 2026

Germany's recycled aluminium production fell 3% year-on-year to 684,564 tonnes in Q1 2026, according to Aluminium Deutschland data. High energy costs, weak demand, and scrap shortages are compounding sector pressure, with 57% of companies not expecting conditions to improve by year-end and 66% rating order situations as poor or very poor.

Novelis angle

Scrap tightness in Germany, where Novelis operates Nachterstedt and other FRP plants, raises competition for post-consumer aluminium and risks increasing closed-loop input costs against the company's 75% recycled-content and 3×30 targets by 2030.
