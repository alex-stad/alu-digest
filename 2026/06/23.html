ElvalHalcor to raise €250m in share capital and invest €455m by 2030

ElvalHalcor, parent company of Greek flat-rolled aluminium producer Elval, announced a €250 million new share capital raise to fund a €455 million investment programme running through 2030, targeting production capacity expansion, aluminium recycling infrastructure, and manufacturing efficiency improvements.

Novelis angle

A €455m capacity push by Elval — one of the largest European FRP producers — signals escalating competition in the packaging and automotive sheet segments where Novelis's Nachterstedt, Pieve Emanuele, and Bresso plants are primary incumbents.

JLR holds annual investor day

At its Gaydon Investor Day on 17 June, JLR announced the Electrified Modular Architecture (EMA) platform underpinning a Range Rover Electric due H2 FY2026-27 (76,976 on its waitlist), Range Rover Sport Electric, electrified Defender and Discovery variants, and the Jaguar Type 01 as Jaguar's first full EV; JLR confirmed five new model launches over 18 months and £3.7 billion in investment by FY27.

Novelis angle

JLR committing five aluminium-intensive EMA models over 18 months is a direct sustained-demand signal for Novelis, whose REALCAR closed-loop partnership supplies BIW sheet to JLR's Solihull and Halewood plants.
