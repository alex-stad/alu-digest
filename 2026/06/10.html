Bauxite curbs, alumina gaps and billion-dollar deals: is aluminium’s supply map about to change?

Global alumina output fell 5.74% month-on-month to 11.92 Mt in April 2026; Guinea’s forthcoming bauxite export controls, Vietnam’s new alumina refinery commissioning, and the EU’s decision to spare Aughinish Alumina from sanctions are together reshaping the upstream supply chain, with pipeline additions offsetting only ~25% of a 730,000-tonne production gap.

Novelis angle

Tighter upstream supply sustains elevated P1020 premiums; Novelis’s ~63% recycled content provides structural insulation from primary aluminium input cost spikes compared with FRP peers more reliant on primary metal.
