Ball Corporation's 2025 Sustainability Report showed 74% recycled content in beverage packaging, with 65% of its aluminium purchased from ASI-certified rolling mills — up sharply from 27% in 2024 — alongside 84% renewable electricity coverage and Scope 1 and 2 emissions 50% below the 2017 baseline.
